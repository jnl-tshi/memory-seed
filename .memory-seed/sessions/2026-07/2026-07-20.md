### Decision

- D: Amended the draft ADR lifecycle sidecar contract. A decision is identified by the pair
  `(source_entry_id, source_decision)` — the entry id fixes the location, so the ordinal need only be
  unique within the entry. The ordinal is **derived** from the DRAFT grammar, never authored: `#### Dn`
